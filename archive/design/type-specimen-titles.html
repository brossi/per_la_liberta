Choosing a title / display face

This judges the display face only — masthead, author line, chapter number, chapter title, and the drop-cap initial all use the candidate; the italic subtitle and body text are a neutral stand-in (Newsreader) so they don't distract. Each block shows a title-page treatment and a chapter opening, at desktop scale and in a 390 px phone frame. The pill marks stroke contrast: we're moving away from Bodoni's high toward moderate / low. Five high-contrast Didone-adjacent faces (Libre Caslon Display, Cormorant, Newsreader Display, Playfair, DM Serif) were checked and excluded — they'd repeat the problem.

Bodoni Moda — currenthigh contrast

The Didone we have. Dramatic thick/thin modulation — beautiful, but the hairlines are exactly the intensity you want to soften.

Alegreyamoderate

A calligraphic old-style designed expressly for literature — warm, with a living movement in the strokes. The most "literary book" of the set: presence and character without Didone drama. True italic and a real small-caps companion (Alegreya SC).
