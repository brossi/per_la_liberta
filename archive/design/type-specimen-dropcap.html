Drop-cap letterform — which face?

The original is a high-contrast Didone (Bodoni). Same versal geometry in every card (≈3 lines); only the initial's face and weight change. The incipit ("L PRESENTE") and body stay Fraunces, exactly as the edition sets them. Compare the stroke contrast and density of the "I" against the original.
