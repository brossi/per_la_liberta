04 — Application guide
ElementColorNotes

Body text #5f5034 Running text. Mimics aged letterpress on body type.

07 — Extraction notes

Methodology: Colors extracted via K-Means clustering (k=4) in CIELAB color space from center-cropped (40%) scans with Gaussian blur pre-processing. The 4th cluster consistently isolated foxing and age-darkened regions, preventing contamination of the primary three values.

Ink density: The "true ink" value (#483826) is sourced from title pages where heavier letterpress strike gives the cleanest read on actual ink pigment. The consensus across all pages (#5f5034) reflects the lighter body-text impression and a century of ink bleed — this is recommended for running text to authentically reproduce the book as it reads today.

Hue axis: The entire palette sits on a single warm axis (~35–40° HSL). There are no cool tones anywhere in this volume. Any blue, violet, or cool gray introduced into the recreation will read as anachronistic.
